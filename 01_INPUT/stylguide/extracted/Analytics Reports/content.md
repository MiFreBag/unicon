Layout - Analytics / Apps
Overview
The analytics page features a full width and full height grid of app cards with 4 columns and variable rows. The entire app card is a hyperlink and 
can be clicked to get to the respective analytics page. If the height exceeds the available screen space, a scrollbar appears.

App cards have two states: Default and disabled. Disabled cards are non-interactive.

scale according to the card width.
